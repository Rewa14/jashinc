JASH Incorporated

Here at JASH Inc, we believe that biodiesel is a healthier solution to decrease our use of oil. For us, the primary advantage of using biodiesel is that it emits 78% less CO2 than normal fuel, drastically decreasing, if not omitting, greenhouse gas emissions that contribute to global warming. Because the production of biodiesel is labor intensive, the growth in demand for this product creates job opportunities and boosts economic growth, particularly in low income areas. A large number of employees, particularly if plants are kept local, are needed to keep up with this market; from growing the crops, to the chemical biofuel creation and, finally, to the distribution of the final product. As a biodiesel research company, JASH Inc is working to answer three questions to help solve the petrofuel problem and issue of climate change: 1) How can biodiesel, in both vegetable and waste form, be incorporated as an alternative to petrofuels, 2) How it can stop the rise in CO2 emission catalyzed climate change, and finally, 3) How it can benefit areas of bioethics as a viable fuel to prompt economic growth, job opportunities, and climate sustainability? We believe that biodiesel is made out of the most efficient combination of resources while still allowing for the environmental benefits and the economic growth that benefits low income communities.
